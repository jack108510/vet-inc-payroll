How many sick days do I get?
Policy Assistant
Full-time staff receive 10 paid sick days per year. Part-time staff receive a pro-rated amount based on hours worked. Sick days can be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or caring for an ill family member. Please notify your manager at least 1 hour before your shift if possible.
You
What's the overtime policy?
Policy Assistant
Overtime is paid at 1.5x your regular rate after 80 hours per pay period (bi-weekly). Any hours worked beyond your scheduled shift must be pre-approved by your manager via an Overtime Request. Unauthorized overtime may not be compensated. Statutory holiday overtime is paid at 2x.
